Peak — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Harris Gore ("I", "me", or "my") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Peak mobile game ("the App").

1. Information I Collect

The App collects the following categories of information:

  • Player name (optional): Entered voluntarily when starting an online multiplayer game. This name is stored temporarily in the game session and is not linked to any account.
  • Online game session data: When using the online multiplayer feature, game state data (board positions, move history, player turn) is stored in Firebase Realtime Database to enable asynchronous play. This data is not linked to any personal identifier.
  • Analytics and crash data: The App uses analytics and crash reporting tools to understand how the game performs and where errors occur. This may include device type, operating system version, crash logs, and gameplay session data. No personally identifiable information is collected through this process.
  • Future — Player rating (ELO-style): A planned feature will track player skill ratings across games. This will be stored anonymously and tied to a randomly generated identifier, not to your name or account.

2. Information I Do Not Collect

  • Your real name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device

3. How I Use Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Enabling online multiplayer gameplay
  • Diagnosing crashes and improving game stability
  • Understanding general usage patterns to improve the game

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Firebase (Realtime Database): Used for online multiplayer game sessions. See firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
  • Analytics / crash reporting: Used for stability monitoring. (This section will be updated with the specific SDK name prior to release.)

5. Data Retention

Online multiplayer game session data is automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity. Analytics and crash data is retained per the third-party provider's standard retention policies (typically 90 days for crash logs).

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users of all ages. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation, and name entry is optional and not stored beyond the current game session. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information beyond what is described in this policy, please contact me immediately.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any game session data associated with a room code by contacting me at the address below. Since the App does not collect personal identifiers, I am unable to retrieve individual records without the specific room code used during play.

8. Security

Game session data is protected by Firebase security rules that restrict read/write access to active game participants only. I take reasonable precautions to protect the information described in this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL and reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
