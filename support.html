Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play online with a friend?
    One player taps "Play Online" and hosts a game to receive a room code. Share that code with your friend — they enter it to join. No account needed.
  • What are the four difficulty levels?
    Easy, Medium, Hard, and Adaptive. Adaptive adjusts its difficulty based on how you've been performing in recent games.
  • My online game disappeared — what happened?
    Online game sessions are automatically deleted after 24 hours of inactivity. If your session expired, simply start a new game.
  • Can I play offline?
    Yes — pass-and-play and vs Computer modes work fully offline. Online multiplayer requires an internet connection.
  • How do I report a bug?
    Please email the address above with a description of the issue and your device model and OS version. Screenshots are helpful if you can include them.
